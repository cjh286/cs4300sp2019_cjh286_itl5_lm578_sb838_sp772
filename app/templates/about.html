About DrinkUp!

DrinkUp! is a searched engine designed for those who want to build their own cocktails using a few set of ingredients that they already own. You can search for an ingredient in our database, and the results will show you a list of ingredients that complement the searched ingredient. The way complements are determined are by co-occurrence. The more times two ingredients appeared together in a recipe, the more likely they co-occur.

In addition, our app provides flavor profiles for each ingredient. These flavor profiles will give you a sense of what your mix will taste like.

The final result, once you finish compiling your ingredients, will be your own cocktail with its own unique flavor profile. We also offer you a list of existing cocktails that use similar ingredients to your original cocktail, so you can try all these out!

How to Use!

There are various ways to search in DrinkUp!

Simple Search

Just type in an ingredient in the search bar, and the autocomplete will show you the list of ingredients that exist in our database. Once you hit search, you will see your query appear under the title "your search:", and you will see the results of your search below that.
You can add to your query by typing in a new ingredient at the search bar on the top, and you can remove items from you query by clicking the little x next to each item in the query.
If you want to clear all query items, simply click the DrinkUp! logo at the top, or press search on an empty search bar.

Advanced Search

If you want to search for a specific type of ingredient, we have labeld all our ingredients as "alcohol", "garnish", or "mixer". To do a labeled search, go back to the very home page with no results. You will see a link to "advanced search" below the search bar. Click on the link, and you will see the option to select what type of ingredient to search for.

Cocktail List

To Add Query to Cocktail List:
Just press on the yellow button with the query word written on it. It will get added to your list of cocktail ingredients.

To Add Searched Ingredient to Cocktail List:
Click on the little green + button next to each ingredient

To Remove Item from Cocktail List:
Click on the red - button next to each ingredient in your cocktail list.

To Remove All Items in Cocktail List:
Click on the red "clear" button at the bottom of the cocktail list.

Cocktail Search

In order to search for cocktails, you need to have a list of ingredients in "Your Recipe." Once you have complied your final list of ingredients, click on the green done button at the bottom of the list to show the taste profile of your cocktail and a list of similar exisitng cocktails.
